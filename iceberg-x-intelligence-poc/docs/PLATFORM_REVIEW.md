# Platform Review

Iceberg X manages R&D missions, internship tasks, mentor assignments, ideas, progress and project outcomes. The M1 POC focuses on making those workflows easier to inspect and hand over.

## Roles

- Admin: creates missions, assigns people, checks readiness.
- Mentor: reviews submissions and publishes feedback.
- Intern: understands mission expectations, gathers evidence and submits deliverables.
- Leadership: needs a fast view of risk, progress and demo readiness.

## Strengths

- Mission-centric operating model is already a strong foundation.
- R&D work can be structured into repeatable phases.
- Mentor review is a natural quality gate.

## Limitations

- Evidence and source quality are not always explicit.
- Submission completeness is hard to compare across missions.
- AI output needs governance before it can influence decisions.
- Handover quality depends on manual discipline.

## Opportunities

- AI-assisted mission drafting.
- Evidence vault with reliability labels.
- Structured submission checklist.
- Readiness score for leadership.
- Handover generator for main team transfer.
